# Batch 8 Index (Episodes 111-120)

## Guests
1. **Mayur Kamat** (Growth, Strategy)
   - *Key Insight:* Speed is a feature; unblock users immediately.
2. **Megan Cook** (Product, Optimization)
   - *Key Insight:* Fix "tax" features (performance/usability) to win back trust.
3. **Melanie Perkins** (Strategy, Mindset)
   - *Key Insight:* Resilience turns rejections into refinement.
4. **Melissa Tan** (Scale, Growth)
   - *Key Insight:* Growth models must evolve; hire for trajectory ("slope").
5. **Melissa Perri** (Strategy, Transformation)
   - *Key Insight:* Product Owner is a Scrum role, not a career ladder.
6. **Melissa Perri + Denise Tilles** (Operations)
   - *Key Insight:* Product Ops enables decisions, it doesn't control them.
7. **Meltem Kuran** (Scale, Speed)
   - *Key Insight:* Measure response times; speed as a cultural value.
8. **Merci Grace** (Leadership, Culture)
   - *Key Insight:* Kindness is truth; ruinous empathy kills teams.
9. **Michael Truell** (AI, Development)
   - *Key Insight:* AI enables spec-driven development.

## Highlights
- **Scaling Pains:** Multiple guests (Melissa Tan, Meltem Kuran) discussed how processes and roles must break and reform during hyper-growth.
- **Role Clarity:** Melissa Perri clarified the specific and often misunderstood distinction between Product Owner and Product Manager.
- **AI & Future:** Michael Truell provided a glimpse into the AI-native developer experience.
